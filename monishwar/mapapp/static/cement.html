Cement Factory

FLSmidth has a facility in Ekhu Nagar, Arakkonam, Tamil Nadu, that was established in 2013 and is a service provider and supplier to the global cement industry. The Arakkonam location focuses on customer service projects like cement plant upgrades, equipment upgrades, and production line overhauls, and it also serves as a manufacturing hub for products like KREBS® pumps for both Indian and global markets. Services and Products: Plant Services: The Arakkonam facility provides services for cement plants, including upgrades, modifications, and overhauls. Equipment Manufacturing: It manufactures equipment such as KREBS® pumps for the mining industry, contributing to FLSmidth's global supply chain. Customer Support: FLSmidth's Arakkonam office is a known name for providing customer-centric services and support.
